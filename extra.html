Student Association Council(SAC)

To ensure and imbibe above skills and sharpening of minds, the students of VIIT have Developed their own student body: “The Student Activity Council (SAC) by the students, for the students and of the students”. The Student Activity Council of members is elected annually from, among the nominees and selections are done through a democratic process involving a panel of faculty members and student representatives drawn from across all departments. It has a President, Vice Presidents, General Secretary, and two Joint Secretaries, supported by a sub-structure of various committees Co-ordinators, who plan, organize and execute a plethora of Co and Extracurricular events and competitions in campus. SAC has emerged truly on the distinct determination and inseparable co-ordination of the students in working towards their success.
